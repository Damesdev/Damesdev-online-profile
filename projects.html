Flashcard Application

Language: Python

I used TKinter, Random, Pandas, and OS modules to complete this flashcard application that has a front and back side with check mark for correct answers and "X" button for incorrect answers and removes correct answers from being used in the future. Starting list of words resets when you have gone through the whole list correctly.
